About Me

I'm a data scientist with a background in statistical modeling, predictive analysis, and hands-on work in Python and R. My experience spans applications in materials science, engineering, and environmental data, often through collaborative research projects. I enjoy bridging theory and practice, whether that's preparing manuscripts, analyzing complex datasets, or sharing results clearly. I hold a PhD in Statistics and an MSc in Pure and Applied Mathematics, and I've had the chance to study and work both in Brazil and abroad, including as a visiting scholar at U.S. universities. I work in both Portuguese (native) and English (advanced).
